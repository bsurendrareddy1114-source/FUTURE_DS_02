# Customer Churn & Retention Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes customer churn behavior and retention patterns for a subscription-based telecommunications business. The dashboard helps identify customers at risk of leaving, key churn drivers, and factors influencing customer retention.

## Objectives

* Analyze customer churn trends and patterns.
* Identify key factors contributing to customer attrition.
* Understand customer lifetime and tenure behavior.
* Discover opportunities to improve customer retention.
* Support business decisions through data-driven insights.

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Total Customers, Churned Customers, Churn Rate %, Average Tenure)
* Customer Churn Distribution Analysis
* Churn by Contract Type
* Churn by Internet Service
* Churn by Payment Method
* Customer Demographic Analysis
* Interactive Filters for Customer Segmentation

## Key Insights

1. The company has approximately **7,043 customers**, with **1,869 customers churned**, resulting in a churn rate of **26.54%**.
2. Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** exhibit the highest churn compared to One-Year and Two-Year contracts.
3. **Fiber Optic** internet users have a significantly higher churn rate than DSL users.
4. Customers using **Electronic Check** as their payment method show the highest churn levels.
5. Customer retention improves with longer tenure, indicating stronger loyalty among long-term customers.

## Recommendations

1. Encourage Month-to-Month customers to switch to long-term contracts through discounts and loyalty programs.
2. Improve customer experience and service quality for Fiber Optic users.
3. Promote automatic payment methods to reduce churn among Electronic Check users.
4. Develop targeted retention campaigns for new and high-risk customers.
5. Continuously monitor churn trends and customer behavior to improve retention strategies.

## Tools Used
